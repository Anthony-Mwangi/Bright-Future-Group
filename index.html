OUR STORY
BRIGHT FUTURE GROUP IN A NUTSHELL
Bright Future was established in late 2008, by two young Saudi’s both with an engineering degree from California State University in Long Beach. They came up with the name way back in campus where as a means to earn extra income they took on a part-time job at the dorms dining hall, where the eldest of the two coined the name that would later become the group’s main seed, representing their pursuit of a bright future. This led them to enter the Food and Beverage industry as their official first step in training. Seven years down the line, the establishment was founded and a year later they opened their first European franchised restaurant. Bright Future Group is a family business with all the board members being family members. The Main objective of the establishment is to compete and lead in the Food and Beverage industry.
OUR MISSION
To become the region’s most successful food and beverage entity by providing innovative and quality products with an outstanding experience to our customers.
